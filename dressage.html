Paige Haas Dressage

I believe in classical dressage training that fosters a partnership with the horse. Whether you just want your trail horse to be more sensitive to the aids, are an eventer wanting to improve your dressage scores, or are interested in pursuing dressage, I would love to help you and your horse achieve your goals.

I have competed through 4th level on self trained horses, earning myself my USDF Bronze Medal and half of my Silver. I am also currently a participant in the USDF L Program. Having completed part 1, I have been accepted into part 2.

I have experience breaking young horses, restarting OTTBs, and working with horses and riders of various skill levels and diciplines. I offer hour long lessons/rides for $45, and full training for $800 per month.
